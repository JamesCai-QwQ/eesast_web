## 团队逻辑

### 接口描述

团队接口的前缀为`/team`。

- `/team/add_team`

- `/team/add_team_member`

- `/team/add_team_player`

- `/team/add_team_code`

- `/team/update_team`

- `/team/update_team_code_name`

- `/team/update_team_player`

- `/team/delete_team_code`

- `/team/delete_team`

- `/team/delete_team_member`

- `/team/delete_team_player`
